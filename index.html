TFIDF Doc summarization

TFIDF or "term frequency, inverse document frequency" is used in text analysis. A word's TFIDF value is often used as a feature for document clustering or classification. The more a term helps to distinguish its enclosing document from other documents, the higher its TFIDF score. As such, words with high TFIDF scores are often very good summarizing keywords for document.
